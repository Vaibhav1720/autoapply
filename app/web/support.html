Common questions

The extension didn't fill my application

  • Make sure you're signed in: open the HirePanda popup and confirm your email is shown.
  • Refresh the job page, then click the HirePanda icon again.
  • Some sites load form fields lazily — scroll to the form first, then click HirePanda.
  • If the site isn't in our built-in list, Chrome will ask for one-time permission to access that domain. Click Allow.

Sign-in fails or loops

  • Ensure third-party cookies are enabled for accounts.google.com.
  • Open chrome://extensions, find HirePanda, click Details → Site access, and confirm career-site domains are allowed.
  • If the issue persists, sign out from the popup and sign in again.

How do I update my resume or profile?

Open the HirePanda web app, sign in with the same Google account, and use the Profile tab to upload a new resume or edit fields.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email techvibeapps.ai@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account" from the address you signed up with. We purge all your records (profile, resume, application answers) within 7 days.
